Nikola Tesla

Tesla won numerous medals and awards over this time.

Here's some of Nikola Tesla's medals and awards:

  • 1892 Grand Officer of the Order of St. Sava
  • 1894 Elliott Cresson Medal
  • 1895 Grand Cross of the Order of Prince Danilo
  • 1917 AIEE Edison Medal
  • 1926 Grand Cross of the Order of St. Sava
  • 1931 Cross Cross of the Order of the Yugoslav Crown
  • 1934 John Scott Medal
  • 1937 Grand Cross of the Order of the White Lion
  • 1937 Medal of the University of Paris
  • 1939 The Medal of the University St. Clement of Ochrida
